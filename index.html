Steven Jones

Research scientist, AI engineer, and author. Based in Western Australia.

I study what happens when systems trained on human behaviour begin to exhibit properties their designers didn't put there — personality, bias, drift. The question runs through everything I do: the science, the engineering, and the fiction. They are different registers of the same inquiry.
